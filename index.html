About Us

Welcome to Charlie Coffee Corner, your neighborhood coffee haven where community, creativity, and caffeine come together. Since opening our doors in 2022, we’ve been proud to serve locally roasted coffee, handcrafted drinks, and fresh, made-from-scratch pastries in a warm and welcoming space. At Charlie Coffee Corner, we believe coffee is more than a drink — it’s a ritual, a connector, and a moment of pause in a busy day. Whether you’re stopping by for your morning latte, settling in to work on your next big idea, or catching up with friends, our shop is designed to be your go-to spot for comfort and inspiration.We’re passionate about sustainability, supporting local suppliers, and creating a space where everyone feels at home. Come in, stay a while, and let us pour you something special.
